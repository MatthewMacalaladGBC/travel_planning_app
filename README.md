## Overview
The Travel Budget Splitting App is a collaborative expense management platform designed to help groups track, split, and settle travel-related costs fairly and transparently. It simplifies financial coordination for trips involving friends, families, or group tours by ensuring everyone knows who paid, who owes what, and how to settle balances efficiently.

## Problem Statement
Group travel often leads to confusion around shared expenses such as accommodation, transportation, food, and activities. Manual tracking is error-prone, time-consuming, and frequently results in disputes.

## Key Features
- Group-based trip creation
- Expense logging with payer and participants
- Automatic equal and custom expense splitting
- Real-time balance calculation
- Category-based expenses
- Settlement summaries
